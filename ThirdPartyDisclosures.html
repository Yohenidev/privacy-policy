Third-Party Disclosures

Effective Date: July 23, 2025

The mobile application “Flagship – Bella Group” (“the App”), developed and published by Technology Partners, LLC, integrates certain third-party services. Below we disclose the services used, the categories of data they process, and their purposes, consistent with our Google Play Data Safety form and Apple App Privacy disclosures.

1. Firebase Analytics (Google LLC)

Firebase Analytics helps us understand how users interact with the App so we can improve performance and user experience.

  • Data Collected: App activity, app interactions, device or other IDs, approximate location (if enabled by user).
  • Purpose: Usage analytics and app performance improvement.
  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

2. Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC)

Firebase Crashlytics provides crash reporting and diagnostics to help us identify and resolve technical issues.

  • Data Collected: Crash logs, app performance data, device model, operating system version, device or other IDs.
  • Purpose: Diagnostics, crash reporting, and app stability improvements.
  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

3. No Other Third-Party SDKs

We do not use advertising, marketing, or unrelated analytics SDKs. If this changes, this page and our disclosures in the Google Play Data Safety form and Apple App Privacy section will be updated.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data is shared only with the third-party providers listed above, and strictly as necessary to support app functionality, analytics, and diagnostics.
