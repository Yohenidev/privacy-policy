Data Safety Policy / Statement

Effective Date: July 23, 2025

This Data Safety Policy explains what data is collected by the mobile application “Flagship – Bella Group” (“the App”), how it is used, and how users may manage or delete their data. This statement reflects the disclosures made in the Google Play Data Safety form and the Apple App Store App Privacy section.

1. Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number (used for account creation, authentication, and support).
  • Vehicle Information: Vehicle details entered or linked by the user.
  • Location Data: Device location (used to provide nearby dealership and service center features).
  • Device and Usage Data: Technical information about your device, app performance, and error logs (collected via Firebase).

2. Purpose of Data Use

  • To provide account-based services and app functionality.
  • To link vehicles to your account and provide related services.
  • To display nearby dealerships and service centers.
  • To improve performance, stability, and user experience (via analytics and crash reporting).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party SDKs and APIs

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google LLC): Collects aggregated usage statistics to improve app features.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC): Collects crash reports and diagnostics to improve stability and security.

For more details, see the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers, marketers, or unrelated third parties. Data is shared only with trusted service providers strictly as necessary for functionality (e.g., Firebase).

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and personal data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated personal data is permanently removed within 30 days from active systems and within 90 days from all backups, except where retention is required by law.

6. Account and Data Deletion

You may delete your account at any time directly within the App by navigating to Account Settings → Delete Account. This will remove your account and personal data as described above. You will receive confirmation in-app and via email. If you need help, you may contact us at support@tppr.com.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to request access to their data, correct inaccuracies, or request deletion at any time. Requests may be made directly in the App or by contacting us.

8. Data Security

All communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Sensitive data is securely handled, and access is limited to authorized personnel only.
