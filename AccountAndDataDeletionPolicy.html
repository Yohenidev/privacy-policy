Account and Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: July 23, 2025

This Account and Data Deletion Policy explains how users of the mobile application “Flagship – Bella Group” (“the App”), published by Technology Partners, LLC, may delete their accounts and associated data.

1. Self-Initiated Account Deletion (In-App)

You can delete your account directly from within the App at any time by navigating to Account Settings and selecting the Delete Account option. No email request is required for standard users.

When you confirm the deletion, you will be logged out immediately and your account will enter the deletion process. A confirmation message will be displayed within the App, and you will also receive an email confirmation sent to the address linked to your account.

2. Data Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted from our systems:

  • Account details (name, email, phone number)
  • Vehicle information linked to your account
  • Authentication data and login credentials
  • App preferences and stored profile information

3. Data Retained

Some data may be retained temporarily or permanently where required by law, regulatory obligations, or legitimate business needs, including:

  • Transaction records (retained for accounting, tax, or compliance purposes)
  • Legal records related to fraud prevention, security, or dispute resolution
  • Aggregated or anonymized usage data that does not identify you personally

Any retained data will be minimized and anonymized wherever possible.

4. Timing of Deletion

Account and data deletion requests initiated in-app are typically processed immediately. Certain data may take up to 30 days to be fully removed from active systems and backups. Backups are systematically purged in accordance with our standard data retention schedule, which is no longer than 90 days.

5. Subscriptions

The App does not provide paid subscriptions or recurring billing features. Therefore, there are no subscription accounts to manage or cancel as part of the deletion process.

6. Alternative Deletion Requests

While the standard method of account deletion is self-service in-app, if you encounter technical difficulties you may also contact us at support@tppr.com. Please include the email address associated with your account for verification.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, use the in-app account management tools or contact us directly. We will respond to valid requests in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
